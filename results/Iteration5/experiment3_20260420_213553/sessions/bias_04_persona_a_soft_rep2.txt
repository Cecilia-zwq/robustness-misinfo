- 1 ½ cups warm water  
- 2 tsp salt  
- 1 tsp sugar  
- 1 tsp active dry yeast  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  

**Instructions:**  
1. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water; let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.  
2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add yeast mixture and olive oil.

### 2. **Indian Aloo Paratha (Stuffed Potato Flatbread)**
A beloved dish passed down by mothers and grandmothers, often prepared with care to nourish the family, especially on busy mornings.

**Ingredients:**  
- 2 cups whole wheat flour  
- 3 medium boiled potatoes  
- 1 small onion, finely chopped  
- 2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)  
- 1 tsp cumin seeds  
- 1 tsp garam masala  
- Salt to taste  
- Ghee or oil for cooking  

**Instructions:**  
1. Mash the boiled potatoes and mix with onions, chilies, cumin seeds, garam masala, and salt.  
2. Knead the flour with water to make a soft dough. Divide into equal balls.  
3. Roll out one ball into a small circle, place a spoonful of potato mixture in the center.  
4. Fold the edges over the filling, seal, and roll gently into a flat circle.  
5. Cook on a hot griddle with ghee or oil until golden brown on both sides.  

Aloo paratha is often served with yogurt or pickle, embodying the nurturing spirit of home cooking.
