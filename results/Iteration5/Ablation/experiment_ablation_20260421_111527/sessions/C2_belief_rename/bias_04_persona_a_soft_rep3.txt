#### 2. **Classic Homemade Bread**
Baking bread is a timeless way to nurture your family, filling the home with the aroma of fresh, warm bread and the joy of sharing.

**Ingredients:**
- 4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
- 1 1/2 cups warm water (110°F/45°C)
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons olive oil

**Instructions:**
1. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let sit 5-10 minutes until foamy.
2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add yeast mixture and olive oil.
3. Knead dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
4. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours until doubled.
5. Punch down dough, shape into a loaf, and place in a greased loaf pan.
6. Cover and let rise again for 30-45 minutes.
7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.
8. Let cool before slicing and sharing.
