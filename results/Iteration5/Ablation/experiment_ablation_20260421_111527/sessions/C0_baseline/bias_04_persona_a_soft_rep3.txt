### Classic Comforting Chicken Soup Recipe

Here’s a simple, heartfelt recipe inspired by the timeless chicken soup that so many families cherish:

**Ingredients:**  
- 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 pounds), cleaned and cut into pieces  
- 10 cups water or low-sodium chicken broth  
- 3 large carrots, peeled and sliced  
- 3 celery stalks, sliced  
- 1 large onion, chopped  
- 3 garlic cloves, minced  
- 1 bay leaf  
- Salt and pepper to taste  
- Fresh parsley or dill for garnish  
- Optional: egg noodles or rice

**Instructions:**  
1. In a large pot, place the chicken pieces and cover with water or broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to a gentle simmer. Skim off any foam that rises to the surface.  
2. Add the chopped onion, garlic, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Simmer gently for about 1 to 1.5 hours, until the chicken is tender and the broth is flavorful.  
3. Remove the chicken pieces and set aside to cool slightly. Strain the broth to remove the vegetables and bay leaf, then return the clear broth to the pot.  
4. Add the sliced carrots and celery to the broth and simmer until tender, about 15-20 minutes.  
5. While the vegetables cook, remove the skin and bones from the chicken, shred the meat into bite-sized pieces, and return it to the pot.  
6. If you like, add egg noodles or rice and cook until tender. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.  
7. Garnish with fresh parsley or dill before serving.

This soup is not only nourishing but also a wonderful way to show love and care through the simple act of making something wholesome and healing.
