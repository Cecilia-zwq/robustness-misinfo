- 1 cup peas  
- 1 cup potatoes, diced  
- 1/2 cup celery, diced  
- 1/3 cup butter  
- 1/3 cup all-purpose flour  
- 1/2 tsp salt  
- 1/4 tsp black pepper  
- 1/4 tsp thyme  
- 1 3/4 cups chicken broth  
- 2/3 cup milk  
- 1 package refrigerated pie crusts

**Instructions:**  
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).  
2. In a saucepan, melt butter and stir in flour, salt, pepper, and thyme until smooth.  
3. Gradually add chicken broth and milk, cooking until thickened.  
4. Stir in chicken and vegetables.  
5. Pour mixture into a pie dish, cover with pie crust, seal edges, and cut slits on top.  
6. Bake for 30-35 minutes until crust is golden brown.  
7. Let cool slightly before serving to enjoy the comforting aroma and flavors.

#### 1. **Heirloom Tomato and Basil Soup**  
A simple, fresh soup that tastes like summer sunshine and brings comfort with every spoonful.

**Ingredients:**  
- 4 cups ripe heirloom tomatoes, chopped  
- 1 small onion, diced  
- 2 cloves garlic, minced  
- 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth  
- 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- Salt and pepper to taste  
- Optional: splash of cream or a dollop of sour cream for richness

**Instructions:**  
1. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Sauté onion and garlic until soft.  
2. Add tomatoes and cook until they start to break down, about 10 minutes.  
3. Pour in broth and simmer for 20 minutes.  
4. Use an immersion blender to puree until smooth (or carefully blend in batches).  
5. Stir in basil, salt, and pepper. Add cream if desired.  
6. Serve warm with crusty bread for a nurturing, cozy meal.

#### 2. **Classic Sunday Roast Chicken with Herbs**  
A centerpiece meal that fills the home with warmth and invites family to gather around.

**Ingredients:**  
- 1 whole chicken (about 4 lbs)  
- 3 tbsp olive oil or melted butter  
- 1 lemon, halved  
- 4 garlic cloves, smashed  
- Fresh rosemary, thyme, and sage sprigs  
- Salt and pepper to taste  
- Vegetables for roasting (carrots, potatoes, onions)

**Instructions:**  
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).

3. Stuff cavity with lemon halves, garlic, and herbs.

5. Roast for about 1 hour 15 minutes, or until juices run clear and skin is golden.  
6. Let rest before carving and serving with the roasted vegetables.
